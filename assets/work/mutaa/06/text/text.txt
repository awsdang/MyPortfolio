Residential 218
Residential 218 consists of 2 storeies on a plot area of 300sqm located in Baghdad, Iraq. the simple facade takes from the heritage of Iraqi architects but in a modernize way, the facade's main feature represent a "Shanshool" or "Meshribia".
This project is a main residential unit on the ground floor with 1 bedroom 2 bathrooms coupled with 2 apartments on the upper floor each has 2 bedrooms, a kitchen living area and bathroom. I worked on designing the project, construction coordination and supervision of the project.
