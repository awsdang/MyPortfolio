Villa 232
As part of my work at Mutaa Al-Hashimi Architects, Villa 232 consists of 3 storeies on a plot area of 1200sqm located in Baghdad, Iraq, the house features 7 rooms, 8 bathrooms, a pool, play area and 4 cars garage.
The complementary design for the villa and the use of gray finish on most of the house. There's an emphasis on the entrance which features a white diamond shaped mass.
